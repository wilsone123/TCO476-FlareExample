San Diego Attractions

There are many places to visit in San Diego. Three of these are Balboa Park, the San Diego Zoo, and Petco Park.

Balboa Park is a 1200-acre park in the middle of downtown San Diego. Inside the park are gardens, walking paths, and more than a dozen museums. Every year the park hosts hundreds of events, including concerts, marathons and races, festivals and parades, and guest speakers.

The San Diego Zoo was founded in 1916 and is located inside of Balboa Park. It is home to more than 3700 rare and endangered animals, and more than 700,000 exotic plants. Among the animals at the zoo are giant pandas, gorillas, koalas, and jaguars.

Petco Park is home to the San Diego Padres, the city's Major League Baseball franchise. The stadium was built in 2004 and is located in downtown San Diego.
